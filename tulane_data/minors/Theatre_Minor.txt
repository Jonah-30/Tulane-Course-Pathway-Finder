MINOR: Theatre Minor
URL: https://catalog.tulane.edu/liberal-arts/theatre-dance/theatre-minor/

=== FULL REQUIREMENTS TEXT ===
The minor in theatre introduces students to the primary areas of theatrical study and activity: acting/directing/performance, design, production, and history/literature. A minimum of fifteen credit hours are required to earn the minor.
