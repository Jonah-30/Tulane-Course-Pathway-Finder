The Mechanical Engineering Minor complements various STEM and non-STEM majors at Tulane by offering a condensed exploration of mechanical systems. It includes the fundamental courses Statics, Dynamics, and Thermodynamics, providing essential knowledge in the field while allowing further exploration via electives. This minor enhances career prospects across industries including aerospace, renewable energy, and manufacturing, preparing students for diverse engineering challenges.
