The impacts of climate change and their local impacts on the region and its peoples stress the need for
an Environmental Studies minor. This minor will utilize university faculty and community partners with a
focus on impacts on local communities and environmental justice.
The EVST minor is comprised of one introductory course, three designated electives, and one capstone
course, for a total of 16 credits. Students focus their study on key environmental issues facing local
communities through the tier-2 writing intensive capstone or tier-2 service learning capstone.
