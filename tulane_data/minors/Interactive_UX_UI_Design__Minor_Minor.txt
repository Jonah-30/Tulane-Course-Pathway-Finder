The mission of the Media + Design program is to provide students with the research and problem-solving skills necessary to create strategic, innovative, cultural and socially significant communication solutions. Students will understand how communication can create change through design, public relations and digital media marketing.
The Interactive Design minor introduces students to the ever-evolving field of interactive design. The minor consists of 18 credit hours, which offer students a foundation in digital design and introduction to interactive design.
