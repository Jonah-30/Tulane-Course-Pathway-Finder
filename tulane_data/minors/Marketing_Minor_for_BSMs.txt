MINOR: Marketing Minor for BSMs
URL: https://catalog.tulane.edu/business/marketing/marketing-minor/

=== FULL REQUIREMENTS TEXT ===
At the undergraduate level, the Freeman School offers a marketing minor for students who are enrolled as business majors. This minor provides BSM students with an understanding of sales and marketing principles used in business and how they relate to other aspects of the business world. Students must complete 9 credit hours, selecting two required classes (6 credit hours) and an additional 3 credit hours chosen from approved electives.
