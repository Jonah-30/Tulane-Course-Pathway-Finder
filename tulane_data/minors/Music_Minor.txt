=== FULL REQUIREMENTS TEXT ===
The Minor in Music is a flexible program that gives students grounding in performance, theory, and musicology while allowing them to cater to their particular music study interests. By choosing from a variety of core courses and electives, students can design their own pathway in Performance, Composition, Musicology, Ethnomusicology, Black American Music, Musical Theatre, or other areas.
