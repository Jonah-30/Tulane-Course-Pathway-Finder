MAJOR: Marketing Major, BSM
URL: https://catalog.tulane.edu/business/marketing/marketing-major/

=== FULL REQUIREMENTS TEXT ===
A major in marketing trains students to analyze marketing problems from an evidence-based perspective and generate well-thought-out, creative solutions. Students gain a comprehensive foundation through courses in marketing research and analytics, consumer behavior, and marketing strategy. Electives provide the option to further specialize in areas such as brand management, analytics or sales.
